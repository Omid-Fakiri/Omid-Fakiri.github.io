TERMINATOR GENISYS

The only good thing (if you want to call it that) about this movie was that Arnold Schwarzenegger returned to play the original Terminator character. However, 3 decades have gone by since the first Terminator movie. So, seeing Arnold looking much older made it even more depressing. It was a cruel reminder that everyone is getting older, and the creation of this story is over 30 years old.

I can't even bring myself to describe to plot of the movie in my own words. You can click here to read up on the details. Believe me, you're head will spin. The abuse of the time-traveling concept clearly goes too far. And, who knows how far this storyline is going to go.
